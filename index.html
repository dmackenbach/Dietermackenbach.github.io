About

I create ceramic works that explore the intersection of form and function, drawing inspiration from natural systems and traditional craft techniques. My practice focuses on hand-built and wheel-thrown pieces that emphasize materiality and the tactile qualities of clay.

Based in Claremont, California, I work primarily with stoneware and porcelain, developing glazes that respond to the unique characteristics of each piece. My work has been exhibited in galleries throughout Southern California.

I hold an MMLIS from USC and currently serve as Social Sciences Librarian at The Claremont Colleges, where I integrate my ceramic practice with my professional work in information science and visual culture.
